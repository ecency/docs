The Ecency **Publish** page provides a rich editor for composing posts. You can drag and drop media, create polls, and style your content with a toolbar or Markdown.

## Adding media

### Images

- Drag and drop images or click the <Badge text="image" /> icon to upload from your device.
- Paste an image link to embed from an external source.

### Videos

- Paste a video URL (YouTube, 3Speak, etc.) and it will embed automatically.
- Use the media upload to attach your own MP4 or WebM files.

## Creating polls

- Select the <Badge text="poll" /> option in the toolbar to insert a poll block.
- Enter your question and up to five answer choices.

## Formatting content

- Use toolbar buttons for headings, lists, quotes, links, and more.
- Switch to Markdown mode for precise control over formatting.
